About BANYAN << HACK >> 2016

Annual Flagship Machine Learning Hackathon


BANYAN << HACK >> 2016 is the annual flagship Machine Learning hackathon for college students. It's organized by Venturesity!

The idea is to help college students get their feet wet into Machine Learning topics.

As you are aware, humanity generates huge amounts of data. A total of 2.5 Quintillion bytes per day! If our math's correct, that works out to about 30 terabytes every second give or take! That’s huuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuuge!!!!!!!!!!!

But how do you aim to make sense of all this data? One way is machine learning. And that’s where you come in. It doesn’t matter if you have no experience in this domain. After all, some of the best discoveries are done by noobs!

For about 86400 seconds (that's 24 hours for you) of hacking, we invite college students all throughout India to come and hack at Banyon Hacks! Give wings to your deep-rooted ideas and show them off to the world!

Tracks

There are 10 major tracks from which to choose. Of course you could mix ‘n match too!

  • 1.Data Visualization

    Discover, model and visualize interesting relationships among attributes of any dataset of your choice. Examples of cool data sets to explore visually are census data, earthquake data, Environ emails etc.

  • 2.Text Mining

    Build your own intelligent chat bot that answers routine queries. Let your bot do the mundane work, while humans do the heavy lifting. Perhaps, eventually, your bot can do it too!

  • 4.Predictive Analytics

    Become the next Oracle. Predict the winners of the next IPL season. If you want to get a bit more serious, try your hand at stock market prediction and walk away a billionaire!!

  • 5.Game AIs

    Build a bigger and better Deep Blue! (Or for you younger folks, AlphaGo!). Beat all human comers (or perhaps other machines) at games: be it board or otherwise.

  • 6.Algorithmic Madness

  • 7.Recommender Systems

    You know how eCommerce websites seem to suggest the best stuff for your needs? You can do it yourself, you know!

  • 8.Image Processing

    Build a cool visual search engine. Or try your hand at filtering out objectionable images and the like. Perhaps even build a QR Code scanner!

  • 9.Audio Processing

    Analyze audio recordings to find your favorite tunes among static noise. Mayhap you can build your own personal assistant like Siri or Cortona.

  • 10.Wildcard

    It's completely open. Hack away at whatever strikes your fancy. Use any APIs you are comfortable with. Build ANYTHING you want.
